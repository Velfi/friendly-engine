## LLM Placement Notes

- Prefer clusters over isolated props: one cart plus crates, sacks, rope, and mud reads better than four unrelated objects.
- Keep large props near paths, doors, and work zones. Keep small props near walls, corners, and foreground framing areas.
- Reuse existing props first, then create missing props only when the scene needs a silhouette or story beat the current catalog cannot cover.
- Vary rotation and scale slightly for repeated clutter, but keep architectural props aligned to walls and openings.
- Use `Core` props for the next screenshot-quality pass before adding `Useful` and `Nice later` details.
